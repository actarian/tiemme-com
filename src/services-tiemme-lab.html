The Company
Tiemme Raccorderie SpA 100% Made in Italy, is a company which was born in the production area of Lumezzane. It is leader in the production of systems and components for the plumbing and heating field and has always offered integrated, highly performing and quick-to-install solutions.

For decades now Tiemme has been producing and introducing the market to its own way of meaning and designing comfort. This has been achieved through a wide range of products, systems, customisable solutions and accessories.

Tiemme’s quality system is a tangible reality which has been certified according to the ISO 9001:2008 standards and is proven by its presence on the international market.

Why Tiemme Lab?
Tiemme Lab is a thermal-technical laboratory where installers, designers, architects and people involved in this field can test the latest solutions of the Tiemme range.

The laboratory was born in the heart of the province of Brescia, right next to Tiemme’s headquarters. It is an innovative training centre which offers different types of courses and training programmes aimed at offering operators of the field an extent and complete knowledge on Tiemme’s systems.

Tiemme Lab is the core of Tiemme's Mission:
Experience and Innovation for a continual growth

Tiemme’s work is inspired by essential values such as environmental responsibility. In an only area, Tiemme Lab concentrates the creation and development of systems offering high energy saving and maximum yield in the long run. Users can test versatile and safe environmental sustainability solutions in Tiemme Lab.

The experimental heart of Tiemme's Systems.
Besides the product, the instruments and all the information for an optimal design and installation.

Tiemme Lab is an idea conceived to concentrate in an only area sustainable solutions and innovative technology at the service of wellness and comfort.

Thermal Technical Academy
All Tiemme Lab’s users and visitors are offered a customised training session on the management of heating and cooling systems, obviously all made in Tiemme.
